# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want dflasher to be a practical, respectful space for people exploring LLM
inference tooling. Be direct, kind, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different levels of experience and hardware access.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence, reproducibility, and tradeoffs.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or publishing private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that make the project
less safe or useful for contributors. Repeated or severe violations may lead to
temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
